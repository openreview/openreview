# Creating an OpenReview Profile

Use these pages to create, activate, and maintain your OpenReview profile.

#### Create and activate

* [Signing up for OpenReview](signing-up-for-openreview.md): Create an account, complete registration, and understand moderation.
* [Resending an activation link](resending-an-activation-link.md): Get a fresh signup/activation link.
* [Expediting Profile Activation](expediting-profile-activation.md): Add and confirm an institutional email to speed activation.

#### Edit your profile details

* [Add or remove a name from your profile](add-or-remove-a-name-from-your-profile.md): Add name variants and set a preferred name.
* [Add or remove an email address from your profile](add-or-remove-an-email-address-from-your-profile.md): Add, confirm, and set your preferred email.
* [Selecting your pronouns](selecting-your-pronouns.md): Set pronouns (including custom values).
* [Finding your profile ID](finding-your-profile-id.md): Locate your profile identifier.
* [Entering Institutional Data](entering-institutional-data.md): Fill education/career history and affiliation fields correctly.

#### Publications and external author pages

* [Importing papers from DBLP](importing-papers-from-dblp.md): Import publications using your persistent DBLP URL.
* [Importing publication data from ORCID to your profile](importing-publication-data-from-orcid-to-your-profile.md): Import externally sourced ORCID records.
* [Manually adding a publication to your profile](manually-adding-a-publication-to-your-profile.md): Upload missing publications directly.
* [Finding and adding a Semantic Scholar URL to your profile](finding-and-adding-a-semantic-scholar-url-to-your-profile.md): Find your author URL and add it to profile links.
* [Finding and adding your ACL Anthology URL to your profile](finding-and-adding-your-acl-anthology-url-to-your-profile.md): Add your ACL Anthology author page URL.

#### Fix duplicates

* [Merging Profiles](merging-profiles.md): Merge accounts if you have multiple profiles.
